ATMA AI is a premier AI consultancy firm that helps businesses automate their workflows and operations.
The company was founded in 2024 with a mission to bring autonomous AI agents to enterprise software.

Our core offering is the Porcupine Orchestrator, an advanced multi-agent system that autonomously routes tasks across different specialized agents. The agents communicate with each other through a centralized SQLite database.

### Core Philosophy & Research Focus
- **Zero Trust AI**: Ensuring enterprise data is never leaked to public models via single-tenant infrastructure and strict RBAC.
- **Neural Pipelines**: Structuring siloed enterprise data using RAG to overcome data debt.
- **Composable AI Architectures**: Restructuring AI workloads into event-driven micro-services to ensure version control, data privacy, and cost predictability.
- **Lean LLM Orchestration**: Favoring targeted routing over massive over-engineered AI systems. Routing tasks based on latency, cost, and compliance needs across models like Claude 3.5, Gemini 2.5, and Llama 3.1.
- **Digital Labor**: Deploying Enterprise Autonomous AI Agents that execute complex workflows with zero human intervention.
- **Global AI Engineering**: Establishing India as the center for enterprise AI engineering and innovation.
